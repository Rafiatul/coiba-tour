Surrounded by some of the best beaches Panama has to offer, these 5 unique Bamboo Villas sit in the heart of Santa Catalina, providing luxurious accommodation that brings the wonders of the natural world right into your life.

Wake up to toucans dining on the natural fruit from trees growing throughout the property, enjoy the tranquil surroundings while still being just a short shuttle ride to the beaches and attractions of Santa Catalina itself.

Comprised of a Master Villa, Private Suites and Dormitory, along with a fabulous Yoga Retreat Center, if a getaway vacation means really getting away and enjoying truly incredible natural environment, then this is the right choice for your accommodation in Santa Catalina, Panama.
